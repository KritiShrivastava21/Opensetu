Privacy Policy

What We Collect

Nothing.
We do not log, store, or monitor any of your DNS queries, IP addresses, or usage patterns. OpenSetu operates as a stateless resolver. We process your request and forget it instantly.

Monetization

We do not monetize this service.
No ads.
No data sales.
No hidden trackers.
OpenSetu is free, and always will be.

Open to All

OpenSetu is an open protocol. Anyone can build and run their own version. We encourage developers, communities, and governments to set up their own resolvers to serve local needs.

No User Accounts

We don’t have user logins or profiles.
We don’t require signups or personal information.
There is nothing to identify you, and that’s intentional.

Anonymous by Architecture

OpenSetu was designed with digital sovereignty and vernacular access in mind. We reject the centralized model of surveillance-based internet infrastructure. Our only job is to translate domain names. Not to observe or analyze who you are.

Legal Compliance

As a resolver, we only redirect domain name requests. We don’t host or serve content, and we don’t control the content that others publish under their domains. We remain compliant with all applicable laws while safeguarding user freedom and privacy.
